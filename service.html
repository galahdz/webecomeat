About or Product

Our meat imitation´s process, benefits and ingredients.

Ingredients

• Wheat flour and water for our seitan base • Consomé • Spinach, rich in iron • Mushroom which has the most protein and vitamins B and D from all vegetables • Lentils which contain in iron and protein • Chick-pea since it has large amounts of minerals like iron and magnesium, as also rich in protein.

How is our meat done?

First, make a mass with flour and water. Second, kneed the dough for at least 10 minutes. Then, let your dough rest in a bowl with water for 2 hours. While the mass rests, prepare the paste. Start with crushing all your boiled ingredients. After that, mix them all together to get a paste. Back to the dough, start washing it with water till the leftover water after washing is completely transparent. This will indicate when our dough is completely clean, leaving carbs and other components, and gluten staying in the mass. When done, continue to add the paste to the cleaned dough. Then, make your meat´s form. After forming the pieces, start boiling your meat pieces for one hour from each side. Finally add oat breaded for a better taste. All ready! Now you can prepare any delicious meal of your preference!!
